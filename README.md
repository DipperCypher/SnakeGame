# 🐍 SnakeGame

SnakeGame brings the classic Snake experience into Minecraft.

Start a game, take control of the snake, and see how long you can survive while collecting food 🍎 and growing longer with every bite. The challenge gets tougher as your snake grows, leaving less room for mistakes and forcing you to plan your turns carefully.

The game takes place on a custom board high above the world ☁️, giving players a clear top-down view of the action. Simple controls make it easy to pick up, while the increasing length of the snake keeps every run exciting.

## ✨ Features

* 🐍 Classic Snake gameplay recreated in Minecraft
* 🎮 Dedicated game board with a top-down perspective
* 🍎 Collect food to grow your snake and increase your score
* 🔄 Easy left and right turn controls
* 💥 Collision-based game over system
* ⚡ Quick setup and instant replayability

## 🎯 Goal

Eat as much food as possible, grow your snake, and achieve the highest score you can before crashing into a wall or yourself.

Whether you're looking for a quick challenge or trying to beat your personal best 🏆, SnakeGame offers a fun arcade-style experience directly inside Minecraft.
